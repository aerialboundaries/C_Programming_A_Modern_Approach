Section 15.1 listed several advantages of diviving a program into multiple
source files.
(a) Describe several other advantages.
(b) Describe some disadvantages.

My Answer:
(a) : 
  1. With "Makefile", the program can be compilied with the files
     which are updated.
  2. We can separate the problem to each files.

(b) :
  1. It will be difficult to understand the whole scheme of the program.
  2. It will be difficult to track the external variables.
  3. Debugging will be difficult.
